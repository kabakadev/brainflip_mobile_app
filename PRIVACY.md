# BrainFlip Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-11-13

BrainFlip uses Firebase services to provide authentication, sync, and storage.

## Data we collect

- **Account data**
  - Email address
  - Display name
  - Profile photo (if using Google sign-in)

- **App usage & study data**
  - Decks and flashcards you create
  - Study progress, streaks, and stats

## How we use this data

- To let you sign in and sync your data
- To track your learning progress
- To improve the app experience (e.g. spaced repetition)

## Data sharing

We do **not** sell your data.

Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud). Public/community decks you choose to share may be visible to other users.

## Your choices

You can:
- Stop using the app at any time
- Request account deletion in a future version, or by contacting the developer
